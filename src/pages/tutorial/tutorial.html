Welcome to Raffles Reads!

Raffles Reads is an app made to encourage Rafflesians to read more english books.

Whats in Raffles Reads?

Raffles Reads has a huge database of Colour Your World with Words articles that you can read in your own time. This is updated weekly by the teachers.

Anything else?

Raffles Reads also automatically finds news articles suited to your interests and displays them to you, so you dont have to spend time on news websites searching articles of interest!

Before you get started...

Please select which categories of news that are of your interest. This will help to tune the displayed articles to be of your interest. This can be found in the news page top left hand corner.

Before you get started...

Swipe right from the left to open up the menu.
You can slide down while on the news page to refresh after updating your preferences.
